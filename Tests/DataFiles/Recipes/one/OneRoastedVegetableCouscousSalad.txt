Roasted Vegetable Couscous Salad

Couscous Salad
Description: 
Notes:
Serves 4
 
Ingredients:
2 blank Potatoes, cubed
1/2 head broccoli, florets
1 blank bell pepper, julianne
2 tbls garlic powder
1 tbls dried parsley
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
3 tbls olive oil
1/2 cup parm cheese
1 cup couscous, cooked
1.5 cups chix stock
1 pad butter
1/2 tbls apple cider vinegar
 
Procedure: 
Preheat the oven to 350 °F
Add the vegetables to the baking sheet, apply oil and 1 tbls garlic powder, parsley, salt, and pepper
Bake until vegetables are tender
Meanwhile, boil stock with salt and butter. Add couscous, remove from heat and cover. Let sit for 5 minutes max
Add the vegies and couscous to large bowl, add parm, 1 tbls garlic powder, apple cider vinegar, toss to combine
